Module Details

Module 1: The Entrepreneurial Journey (3 Months)

Start with 500 essential business and startup vocabulary terms. This module builds your foundation in modern entrepreneurship—from idea generation to team building and funding. You’ll explore how startups are formed, how business models are validated, and what it takes to turn an idea into a scalable product or service.

  • Vocabulary for business, startups, and investment
  • Idea validation, research, and planning
  • Company formation and legal basics
  • Building effective teams
  • Introduction to startup finance and sales strategy

Module 2: Communication & Presentations (1 Month)

Sharpen your ability to express ideas clearly and professionally in English. Learn to structure compelling emails, introduce yourself confidently, network effectively, and deliver presentations that leave an impact.

  • Professional email writing
  • Introducing yourself in business settings
  • Storytelling in presentations
  • Vocabulary for meetings and teamwork
  • Visual & spoken presentation techniques

Module 3: Negotiation & Investor Pitching (1 Month)

Become a persuasive communicator. Practice real-world negotiation scenarios and learn how to pitch your startup idea to investors or partners—all in clear, powerful English.

  • Vocabulary for finance, deals, and persuasion
  • How to structure and deliver an investor pitch
  • Handling objections and negotiating terms
  • Verbal confidence in high-stakes conversations
  • Pitch deck writing and structure

Module 4: Branding & LinkedIn Presence (1 Month)

Build your digital identity and brand like a pro. From crafting a standout LinkedIn profile to developing a unique personal and business brand, this module positions you to be noticed.

  • Personal branding principles
  • LinkedIn setup and optimization
  • Elevator pitch development
  • Visual identity and tone of voice
  • Branding for solopreneurs and startups

Module 5: Website & SEO for English Markets (1 Month)

Learn to build and optimize a professional English-language website. Whether it’s a startup landing page or personal portfolio, reach your audience with clarity and impact.

  • Structuring web content for clarity
  • Writing with SEO best practices
  • Optimizing for mobile and speed
  • Creating credibility through design
  • Comparing builders vs. custom design

Module 6: Final Business Pitch Presentation (1 Month)

Bring it all together. Develop and present your full business plan live, using everything you’ve learned—communication, branding, negotiation, and pitching skills.

  • Write and present a full business plan
  • Deliver a live final pitch to instructors
  • Receive feedback and certification
  • Build confidence through real-world simulation
  • Celebrate your growth at graduation
